# Privacy Policy for Psyke

Effective date: March 26, 2026

Psyke is a tabletop companion app for tracking Sanity, Fears, Madness, Wounds, and Spells.

## Information We Collect

Psyke does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app stores the game data you enter, such as tracker values and related notes, locally on your device so your progress can persist between sessions.

## How Your Information Is Used

The information entered into Psyke is used only to provide the app's core functionality, including saving and restoring your local game state.

## Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area. The app does not upload your saved data to a remote server.

## Data Sharing

Psyke does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

## Advertising and Analytics

Psyke does not include third-party advertising SDKs or analytics services.

## Permissions

Psyke is designed to operate without requesting sensitive Android permissions for its core functionality.

## Children's Privacy

Psyke is not directed to children under 13, and the app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep app data limited to local device storage. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and use of the app is at your own discretion.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the effective date in this document.
